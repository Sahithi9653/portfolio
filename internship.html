Devops



Objective:
    In simple terms, DevOps is a software development approach that emphasizes collaboration and integration between development (Dev) and operations (Ops) teams. It aims to streamline the software development lifecycle by breaking down the barriers between these traditionally separate functions. The main goal of DevOps is to deliver software faster and more reliably, ensuring that development and operations teams work together seamlessly to build, test, deploy, and maintain applications. By promoting automation, continuous integration, continuous delivery, and monitoring, DevOps fosters a culture of communication, feedback, and continuous improvement, leading to higher-quality software and more efficient development processes.
Duration:
    6weeks
